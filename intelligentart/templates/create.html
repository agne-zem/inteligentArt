Before generating, we need to test out the settings. Image generation process takes some time, so first we check if you like your settings. The test will give you a smaller sample generated image with your settings and two other images with randomly selected ones. After that you can choose from those images and generate a bigger picture or you can try out other settings and test again.

Keep in mind that some of the content image small details (for example, a person standing far away, window of a big house) are lost because of how small the sample is. Generating bigger image will most likely have the smaller details. Testing is used for getting the general idea of the chosen settings.

Please wait, this will take up to a minute.
